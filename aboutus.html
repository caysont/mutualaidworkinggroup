ABOUT US

The Mutual Aid Working Group (MAWG)

We are a collective of students and community members coming together to work on projects that benefit the community as a whole. Formed through the Campus Y Executive Board, we are a social justice group seeking collective liberation through genuine community and connection.

The Chapel Hill Community Fridge

Based on a model by Durham Community Fridges, our fridge allows people to go find fresh food at no cost. This initiative allows for community members to connect with one another and stay healthy. We keep us safe.
